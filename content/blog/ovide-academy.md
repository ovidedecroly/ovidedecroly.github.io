# What is Ovide Academy?

**Ovide Academy** is the personal academic website of **Dr. Ovide Decroly Wisnu Ardhi**. This website is developed as a space for sharing research notes, teaching materials, blog articles, books, and applied AI ideas.

The main focus of Ovide Academy includes applied artificial intelligence, computer vision, deep learning, academic writing, and digital education.

## Why Ovide Academy?

Ovide Academy is designed to become a personal knowledge hub. It connects academic research, teaching materials, and practical AI discussions in one website.

The website is especially focused on:

- Applied Artificial Intelligence
- Computer Vision
- Deep Learning
- Object Detection
- YOLO-based Detection
- Academic Writing
- Digital Education
- Books and Learning Materials

## About Dr. Ovide Decroly Wisnu Ardhi

Dr. Ovide Decroly Wisnu Ardhi is an Applied AI Researcher with interests in computer vision, deep learning, visual intelligence systems, academic writing, and digital education.

Through Ovide Academy, he shares academic notes, learning resources, and research-based ideas for students, researchers, educators, and general readers interested in applied AI.
